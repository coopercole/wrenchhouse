ABOUT

wrench fund is run by five uwaterloo engineering grads with the goal of increasing the agency of young engineers and the amount of hardware built in canada

THE TORQUE GRANT

wrench fund is offering a $300 grant to a uwaterloo capstone team to help them overcome financial barriers and inspire them to pursue their project further

WHAT WE ARE LOOKING FOR

we want to support teams that are:

  • passionate about solving problems
  • curious about commercialization and taking their project to the next level
